About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Quantitative and Computational Biology ( QCB Graduate Program ), working at the Storey Lab for Statistical Genomics in the Lewis-Sigler Institute for Integrative Genomics, Princeton University.

I received my Master of Arts in Quantitative and Computational Biology at Princeton in 2021, completed my Graduate Certificate in Statistics and Machine Learning in 2022 at Princeton CSML.

Prior to joining Princeton, I received my Bachelor of Science and Economics from Peking University in July 2017, received my Master of Science from Department of Biostatistics at Harvard University in May 2019.

Research Interest

My major research interests lie in developing statistical and computational methods to measure complex biological system, especially focusing on
